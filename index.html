What This Means

A 67.0% dismissal rate means roughly 67 out of every 100 Chapter 13 cases in the N.D. Ill. end without a discharge. Dismissed debtors lose automatic stay protection, forfeit fees paid, and often end up worse off than before filing.

High dismissal rates can signal systemic problems -- inadequate attorney preparation, unrealistic plans, or high-volume practices prioritizing filing speed over case quality.
